About Me

Hello! I’m Praful Peethambaran Nair, a recent graduate from Concordia University with a Master of Applied Computer Science. I have a strong passion for creating innovative applications and tackling challenging problems. My expertise lies in data analysis, software development, and programming. I thrive in environments that challenge my problem-solving skills and allow me to work on impactful projects. Whether it's developing user-friendly applications, analyzing complex datasets, or automating processes, I am driven by a desire to make technology work seamlessly for users. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends. I'm also a food enthusiast who loves discovering new culinary delights, and Poutine holds a special place in my heart! I am excited to bring my skills and passion to new opportunities and look forward to connecting with like-minded professionals. Let’s create something amazing together!
